Important note about COVID-19

The spread of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) has introduced much uncertainty in our day to day lives. We are all runners at heart and want to maintain our hard-won training and fitness. Group running presents some unique factors to consider. Running with friends is motivating, especially with goal races getting cancelled and we all know what a fun and welcoming group the RunningRats are. However, prolonged exercise in proximity to others adds a transmission risk.

The Running Room will continue their Run Club and Clinics. The difference is the Rats are typically a larger group and we have had the fortune of seeing 30+ runners on most runs. With that consideration and to help mitigate the health risk to our members, the RunningRats will be pausing our Tuesday runs until further notice. As the situation is developing rapidly, we will be regularly assessing to determine when we should run together again.

In the meantime, continue sharing your workouts online by tagging your Instagram workouts with @runningratsto and join the RunningRats Strava Group.

Here are some helpful things to consider if you are running in a group:

  • try to maintain 2m/6ft separation between runners
  • avoid physical contact, including high-fives and hugs
  • meet up outdoors or in less confined spaces
  • avoid lingering around after the run and shower asap
  • avoid sharing water and food
  • if you have any mild cold/flu symptoms, avoid group running entirely
  • no snot rockets!
